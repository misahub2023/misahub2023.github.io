Dr. Nidhi Goel

Prof. Nidhi Goel is working as Professor in the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering Department at Indira Gandhi Delhi Technical University, New Delhi. She has been heading the ECE Department since May 2018. Prior to joining IGDTUW, she was associated with Delhi Technical University (Formerly, DCE).

She has teaching and research experience of more than 20 years. She received her B.E. Degree in Electronics and Communication and M.Tech. Degree in Digital Communication. She pursued her research interest and completed her Ph.D. from Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee, India.

Her area of interest includes Medical Image Processing, Deep Learning, Artificial Intelligence applications, Multimedia Security, Computer Vision, Soft Computing Techniques.
