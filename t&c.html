Terms and Conditions (T&C)

Misahub stands for Medical Imaging and Signal Analysis Hub. Misahub is a virtual, open-source hub which aims to research, analyze and develop easy-to-use healthcare solutions with the help of emerging technologies like artificial intelligence, website development, android development, cloud computing, block chain etc. It comprises of research students, faculty, doctors working in the field of AI in healthcare. MISAHUB was functional from 2019 to 2024 and no longer takes new mentees. All the projects, code files, and datasets are available for resource and further use in research.

The work under Misahub comes from different research teams. As a result, the limitations and the rights asserted on datasets or code or research work vary.
